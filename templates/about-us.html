Discover Our Story

Humble Beginnings

A Family Tradition

SpiceJar began in my grandmother's kitchen in 2010, where she perfected her pickle recipes over decades. What started as a way to preserve seasonal produce became a beloved family tradition, with each batch carefully crafted using time-honored techniques.

I remember watching her select the freshest mangoes, carefully measuring spices, and patiently waiting for the flavors to mature. That attention to detail and respect for tradition became the foundation of SpiceJar.

The First Commercial Batch

In 2015, after years of friends and family begging for jars of our pickles, we decided to take the leap. With just 50 jars of our signature mango pickle, we debuted at a local farmers market.

To our amazement, we sold out in just two hours! The demand was overwhelming, and we knew we had something special. That first market day gave us the confidence to turn our passion into a business.

Our Growth Journey

2015

Farmers Market Debut

Launched with just one product at the local farmers market. Sold all 50 jars in our first day!

2016

First Retail Partnership

Local gourmet store picked up our products. Expanded to 5 varieties including lemon and mixed vegetable pickles.

2017

Commercial Kitchen

Moved operations from home kitchen to a certified commercial space. Hired first two employees.

2018

Online Store Launch

Began shipping nationwide through our e-commerce platform. Added 3 new spicy varieties to our lineup.

2019

First International Order

Shipped our first international order to the UK. Expanded product line to include pickle gift sets.

2020

Retail Expansion

Our products now available in 50+ specialty stores across the country. Launched our first subscription service.

2022

New Facility

Moved to a 10,000 sq ft production facility. Team grew to 15 employees. Added 5 new regional specialty pickles.

2023

International Distribution

Now shipping to 12 countries worldwide. Featured in Food & Wine magazine's "Best Artisanal Foods" list.

Our Growing Reach

From Local to Global

What began as a local farmers market stall has grown into an international business with customers across four continents. We're proud to share our traditional flavors while adapting to new markets.

Our expansion has been carefully managed to maintain the quality and authenticity that made us successful. Each new market is researched to understand local tastes while staying true to our roots.
